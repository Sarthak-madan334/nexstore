NexStore ⚡
A premium electronics storefront with a built-in AI shopping assistant — fast, opinionated, and ready to deploy.

What It Is
NexStore is a fully client-side React storefront for a fictional electronics brand. It's got the things a real store needs — browsing, filtering, a cart, a wishlist, a flash sale — plus a conversational AI assistant powered by Claude that can answer product questions, compare specs, and help shoppers decide.
It's built to look sharp and feel snappy. Swap in your own products and branding and it becomes a real thing.

Features

8 products across 5 categories — AUDIO, COMPUTE, WEARABLE, TIMEPIECE, INPUT
Product modal with image zoom and gallery
Cart drawer with quantity controls and live totals
Wishlist with persistent state
AI chat assistant — Claude-powered, knows the product catalog
Flash sale countdown timer — animated, pressure-inducing
Animated hero canvas — makes the landing page feel alive
Confetti on add-to-cart — small joy, big impact
